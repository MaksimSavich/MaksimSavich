About Me

My name is Maksim Peter Savich and I was born on July 12th 2004. I am the youngest child of three children and I am second generation here in the United States of America. My grandparents came here from a country called Serbia in Europe and my parents are both first generation born here in Michigan. I grew up in White Lake Michigan and still live here to this day. As I grew up I began to play my most favorite sport, hockey, at the age of three. I soon chose to become a goalie and I have persued that position for my whole hockey career.
